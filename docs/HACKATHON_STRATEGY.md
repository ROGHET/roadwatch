\# RoadWatch Winning Strategy



Primary judging targets:



1\. Data Accuracy

2\. Complaint Routing

3\. Budget Transparency

4\. UI/UX

5\. Global Applicability



Must demonstrate:



\- Real road records

\- Real contractor records

\- Budget sanctioned vs spent

\- Authority routing

\- AI assistant

\- Safety alerts

\- Multilingual support



Demo priority:



Road → Budget → Contractor → AI Query → Complaint → Routing → Dashboard



Never cut:



\- Road details

\- Complaint routing

\- Budget transparency

\- AI assistant
