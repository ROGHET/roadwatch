\# RoadWatch



Road Safety Hackathon 2026

IIT Madras CoERS



\## Objective



AI-powered citizen transparency platform for roads.



Users can:



\- View road information

\- Track budgets

\- Track contractors

\- Report road issues

\- Route complaints

\- Ask road-related questions

\- Receive safety alerts



\---



\## Core Features



\### Feature 1



Interactive Road Map



Technology:



\- Leaflet

\- OpenStreetMap



Capabilities:



\- Search roads

\- Click roads

\- View road information
