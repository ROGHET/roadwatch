# Required Datasets

To reach a 100% real-data system, RoadWatch still needs:

- Road segment geometries with official road IDs.
- Road ownership by segment.
- Authority jurisdiction polygons.
- Department routing rules by road class and geography.
- Contractor assignment by road, package, and period.
- Tender award records.
- Contractor performance and penalty records.
- Work order records with start date, expected completion date, and actual completion date.
- Road inspection and quality audit records.
- Maintenance history by road segment.
- Pothole and defect survey records.
- Complaint records from the production PostgreSQL database or official grievance systems.
- Complaint status history and closure timestamps.
- Accident records with coordinates, road ID, severity, injuries, and fatalities.
- State/city aggregate accident records for trend context.
- Budget sanctioned by project, road, package, department, and financial year.
- Budget released by project and financial year.
- Budget spent by project and financial year.
- Remaining balance by project and financial year.
- Payment voucher or bill records for works.
- Physical progress or work-completion percentages from official measurement books.
- Tender compliance and bid comparison datasets.
- Audit observations from CAG, municipal audits, or departmental vigilance reports.
- Road condition sensor or survey datasets.
- Traffic telemetry by segment or junction.
- Weather and rainfall observations by location.
- AQI observations by location.
- OpenStreetMap or official GIS basemap extracts for road geometry.
- Ward, district, city, and state boundary datasets.
- Municipal road inventory datasets for BMC and other cities.
- Tamil Nadu Highways structured road/work datasets.
- CRIF structured project release datasets.
- NCRB or iRAD location-level accident datasets.
